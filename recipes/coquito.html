Coquito

Description

Coquito is a Puerto Rican rum and coconut drink that's blended for a rich, creamy, and delicious treat. My grandmother and mother used to prepare it during the Christmas season as I was growing up, and the tradition has stayed with me!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 cup dark rum
  • 2 cans coconut milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt

Steps

  1. Pour the water, milk, rum, and coconut milk into a blender.
  2. Add the cinnamon and salt.
  3. Blend until well combined, then refrigerate for at least one hour, or until ready to serve.
